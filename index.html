How the math works. A persistent-state slot goes +EV only when someone has built the meter close enough to its guaranteed trigger that the feature you collect outweighs the base-game bleed getting there. Counter games: EV = (feature × bet) − [(ceiling − meter) ÷ (2 × accrual)] × bet × (1 − base RTP). The break-even meter is where that hits zero.

Trust the structure, verify the constants. Resets and ceilings here are the hard-to-find facts and are reliable. Only Buffalo Link ships fully calibrated (1.7 heads/spin, ~85% base return, 20–30 unit feature). For every other game the accrual and feature values load as estimates — dial them to the real machine or a verified guide before trusting the number.

Reality check. +EV is a long-run average; individual sessions swing hard and the feature often pays near zero. Need a bankroll that absorbs it, and the good states get locked up fast by other players. If a session drifts from "playing only +EV states" into "playing because I'm here," that's the stop-loss — walk. For informational use; verify every number yourself.
